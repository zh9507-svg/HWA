Current State
Greenfield Build — Starting from Zero

The Sales Process
12-Stage Lead Journey
1

Lead comes in via Facebook/Instagram Ad, Google Ad, or Website Contact Form

2

Lead enters the pipeline in New Lead and the team attempts contact within 5 minutes

3

If outreach starts, move to Contact Attempted; if unanswered, route to No Answer

4

No-answer leads enter Follow Up Sequence Active and receive service-specific SMS/email + before/after proof

5

If the client is interested but not yet booked, move to Appointment Requested/Pending

6

Booked clients move into Booked Appointment and receive reminder messaging

7

Attended visits are marked Arrived at Salon for fulfillment and post-visit follow-up

8

Operational exceptions are handled via No show, Reschedule, and Incorrect number

9

Non-buyers are categorized as Lead Not Interested or Not eligible due to medical condition

10

Any lead who still does not respond after the sequence is parked in No Answer for reactivation logic

11

Post-visit review, rebooking, and nurture messaging fire after attended appointments

12

Active and lost leads remain segmented for future promotions, before/after proof, and reactivation outreach

⚠
Critical Context — Read Before Building

This is a trust-first business. Dr. Foye's reputation is everything. Every automated message must feel like it came from her team directly — warm, professional, and human. Speed matters: first SMS + email within 5 minutes of a new lead. Personalization matters: every service gets its own message set so a Botox inquiry never gets a Hair Removal email.

Section 03

Every Workflow That Needs to Be Built

8 workflows. Each built as a separate GHL automation. Label them exactly as named.

📲
Workflow 01
New Lead Welcome (Facebook/Instagram)
Trigger: Meta lead form submitted
  • →Create contact → move to Stage 1
  • →Tag "source: Facebook Ad"
  • →Identify service interest from form field
  • →Route to service-specific sub-workflow
  • →Notify front desk via internal notification
  • →Wait 2 minutes → Send SMS #1 (service-personalized) + Email #1 (service-personalized)
🌐
Workflow 02
New Lead Welcome (Google / Website)
Trigger: Website or landing page form submitted
  • →Create contact → move to Stage 1
  • →Tag "source: Google/Website"
  • →Identify service interest → route to service-specific sub-workflow
  • →Notify team via internal notification
  • →Wait 2 minutes → Send SMS #1 (website variant) + Email #1 (website variant)
Service Routing Note: Each of the 11 services has its own dedicated booking link stored as a Custom Value. The welcome message must dynamically insert the correct {{custom_values.booking_link__[service]}} based on what the lead expressed interest in.
🔁
Workflow 03
No Answer Follow-Up Sequence
Trigger: Lead moved to Stage 2
  • →Immediately → SMS #2 + Email #2 (service-personalized, personal tone from Dr. Foye's team)
  • →Wait 24 hours → SMS #3 + Email #3 (social proof message)
  • →Wait 48 hours → SMS #4 + Email #4 (final attempt, booking link)
  • →Wait 48 hours → no response → move to Stage 9, tag "No Response - [Service]"
Stop Condition: Any reply OR booking → pause sequence immediately → move to Stage 4.
📅
Workflow 04
Appointment Reminder Sequence
Trigger: Appointment booked via any service calendar (Stage 5)
  • →Immediately → SMS confirmation + Email confirmation (includes service name, date, time, location)
  • →24 hours before → SMS reminder + Email reminder
  • →2 hours before → SMS final reminder
  • →60 min after appointment time with no stage change → no-show SMS + email fires
🔄
Workflow 05
No-Show Re-booking
Trigger: Appointment marked No-Show OR 60 min past appointment with no stage change
  • →Immediately → SMS #5 + Email #5 (warm, zero guilt, reschedule link)
  • →Wait 24 hours → SMS #6 + Email #6 (second gentle attempt)
  • →Wait 48 hours → no rebooking → move to Stage 9, tag "No Show Lost"
⭐
Workflow 06
Post-Visit: Review + Rebooking
Trigger: Lead moved to Stage 8 (Won)
  • →Immediately → SMS thank you + Email thank you (personalized by service)
  • →Wait 3 days → SMS review request + Email review request (Google Review link)
  • →Wait 7 days → SMS rebooking nudge + Email with next recommended treatment
♻️
Workflow 07
30-Day Reactivation
Trigger: Contact has been in Stage 9 for 30 days
  • →Day 30 → SMS promo offer + Email with before/after social proof (service-relevant)
  • →Day 37 → no response → follow-up Email with limited-time offer
  • →Day 44 → final SMS + Email: "Last chance" message
  • →After final attempt → tag "Reactivation Exhausted"
📣
Workflow 08
Monthly Broadcast (Existing Clients)
Trigger: Manual send or scheduled monthly
  • →Send branded email newsletter with current promos, new services, and a rebooking CTA
  • →SMS version: short teaser + link
Segments: Active Clients · Lapsed Clients (Stage 9) · Inquiry-Only (never visited)
